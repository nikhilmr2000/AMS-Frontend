The Ultimate all-in-one Web-App for Attendance System

Attendance Management System ( A | M | S ) is generally a all-in-one app which provides all the services for attendance management. A | M | S also helps in tracking the details of the teachers, professors and other faculties and also helps in managing the details of the students and faculties working within the organization.


Aspects of Attendance Management System ( A | M | S )

Dashboard

Dashboard helps to navigate the details of the students, faculties and other staffs working in the organisation. Dashboard helps admin to have control and access to the A | M | S throughout. Dashboard helps in knowing the changes happening in an organisation.

Dashboard

Organisation

Organisation helps to find the attendance records of the students in the organisation. A | M | S not only helps to find the record of the students but also helps to know the details of the faculties and other staffs within the organisation.

Organisation

Profile Status

Profile Status is a feature in A | M | S which helps to know the current status of the students or faculties working in the organisation. This helps the faculties or the students to update their details in the organisation.
